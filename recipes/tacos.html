Tacos

Description

A Mexican staple made with soft or crispy tortillas filled with various meats, veggies, and toppings. Versatile and flavorful!

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 500g ground beef or shredded chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook onion and garlic in a pan until softened.
  2. Add ground beef or chicken, cook until browned.
  3. Season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Warm tortillas in a pan or oven.
  5. Assemble tacos by layering meat, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, salsa, and cilantro.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
