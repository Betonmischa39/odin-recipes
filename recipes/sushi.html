Sushi

Description

Nigiri sushi is hand-pressed sushi rice topped with a slice of fresh fish or seafood. Simple, elegant, and a sushi classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Fresh fish slices (e.g., salmon, tuna, shrimp)
  • Wasabi (optional)
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger (for serving)

Steps

  1. Rinse and cook sushi rice with water. Let cool slightly.
  2. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into the rice gently.
  3. Wet your hands with water to prevent sticking.
  4. Take a small handful of rice and shape it into an oval mound (about 2-3 inches long).
  5. Optionally, smear a small amount of wasabi on top of the rice.
  6. Place a slice of fresh fish on top, gently pressing to adhere.
  7. Serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger on the side.
