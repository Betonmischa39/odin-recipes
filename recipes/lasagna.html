Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese. Perfect for family dinners and leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • 100g grated parmesan
  • For béchamel: 50g butter, 50g flour, 500ml milk, nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 20–30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Prepare béchamel: Melt butter, whisk in flour, then slowly add milk. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  6. Layer in a baking dish: meat sauce, noodles, béchamel, mozzarella. Repeat 2–3 times.
  7. Top with parmesan. Bake for 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
